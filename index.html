About me

My name is Landon Voss and incredibly excited to be taking Accessible Computing here at A&M this semester. I'm a CS senior, come from Cypress, Texas and hope to one day work with developing emerging VR technologies with accessibility in mind.

Accessible Technology

My interests in accessible technology revolve around the idea that everyone should be able to interact with the everyday technologies that are actively changing our world for the better. I'm especially interested in better understanding those with visual and motor impairments and the various ways that they must interact with daily technologies that non-disabled people don't ever have to think about.
